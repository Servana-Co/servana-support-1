Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Servanaa account and all associated personal data directly from the app:

  • Open the Servanaa App.
  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • Confirm your request and your account will be permanently deleted.

Data that will be deleted: your profile information, saved addresses, reservations, and reviews.

Data that may be retained: certain transaction records (e.g., invoices and payment confirmations) may be kept for up to 12 months as required by legal, accounting, or regulatory obligations. After this period, the data will be permanently erased.

If you experience any issues deleting your account, please contact us at support@servanaa.com.
